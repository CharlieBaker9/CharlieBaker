ABOUT ME

Hello My Name is Charlie! I am a 24 at Dartmouth College. I was introduced to computer science in the winter of 2021 and have loved it. I have learned python, java, and have had a brief introduction to html. This is my first website, however, I am a creator at heart so I've been designing projects all my life. The second page contains just a few of my favorite art projects I have completed over the years. I love 3D art because your audience gets to engage with the final product. The same is true for web design. Everyone interacts with art and websites differently, but there are patterned responses that you can pick up and use to your advantage. Through understanding how someone will interact with your final product you can take it to the next level. I am excited to work with DALI to stretch myself as a coder, but also to be part of a great community of fellow coders. The community/team aspect of DALI really speaks to me and the way I like to live my life. I rely on others for so much, just as they rely on me.
